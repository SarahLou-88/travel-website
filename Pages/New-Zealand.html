The Ranch

I spent six unforgettable months working on a horse ranch nestled in the Grampian Mountains of Victoria, Australia. My days were filled with the rhythm of hooves and the fresh scent of eucalyptus, as I taught people how to ride horses and led pack rides through the breathtaking mountain trails.

Visited the 12 Apostles which are a series of limestone stacks located off the coast of Port Campbell National Park, along the Great Ocean Road.

I celebrated Australia Day with friends, enjoying a classic Aussie barbecue, playing backyard cricket, and soaking up the summer sun. Australia Day, held on January 26th, marks the arrival of the First Fleet in 1788 and the beginning of British settlement.
